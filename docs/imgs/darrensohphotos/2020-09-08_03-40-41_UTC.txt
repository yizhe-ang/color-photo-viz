@Apple Marina Bay Sands opens to the public this Thursday 10 September. Designed by Apple in collaboration with Fosters + Partners, the store features an underground avenue clad with the now iconic stonework that adorns many Apple stores around the world. The store also features a glass sphere with an oculus at the apex of the dome set entirely in @marinabaysg
