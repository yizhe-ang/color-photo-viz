This lesser known Singapore Improvement Trust built tenement is on Madras Street between Serangoon Road and Jalan Besar, the area commonly known as Little India. Built in 1940 as an infill for a vacant plot in an area completely full of shophouses, it was at some point after the war known as Madras Road Estate. Today it is the @thegreatmadras boutique hotel and in 2018, @farm.sg who did the A&A works and the owners won the Architectural Heritage Awards from the @urasingapore
.
