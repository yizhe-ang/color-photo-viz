Blocks 160 and 161 Mei Ling Street in Queenstown Singapore.
- First of two "point blocks" actually built by the HDB
- Completed in 1969 and sold to the public in 1970.
- Actually consists of THREE point blocks joined together
- One super-block with three separate lift lobbies - Each lift lobby offers access to 4 units per floor
- 3 3-Room and 1 4-Room unit on each floor - 20 storeys in height for both blocks 160 and 161
- Total of 342 3-room units and 114 4-room units
- HDB never built point blocks like these two ever again
