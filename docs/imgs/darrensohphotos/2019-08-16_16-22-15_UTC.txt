Offerings and candles being lit at a junction within the Choa Chu Kang Chinese Cemetery last night, on the 15th Day of the Lunar 7th month as family members pay their respects to the departed.

The three white signs in the photo are notices put up by the National Environment Agency to inform the public and families of the exhumation schedules of the various plots.
